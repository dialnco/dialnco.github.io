Diego Cortez Lopez

Psych and tech

"Learning never exhausts the mind." — Leonardo da Vinci

I'm interested on Human-Computer Interaction. Specifically, on the use of games and different technologies for learning/training purposes and as measurement instruments. I believe that technology and games are essential for the engagement and development of powerful citizen science projects. I think that will be the future of research in social sciences.
I'm also interested on the study of social perception on Virtual Reality environments, and the interaction with simulated Social AI. That is, the application of Artificial Intelligence to games.


On other topics, I currently contribute and work on active projects on: Mental models, decision-making, work and happiness/life satisfaction, and Well-Being and HR.
